About the Artist

Welcome to ZOMBIELANDWRLD, a creative space where the eerie beauty of the post-apocalyptic world comes to life on canvas. I'm Kira, the artist behind these works, and I'm passionate about exploring themes of decay, survival, and the strange beauty that can emerge from chaos.

My artistic journey began with learning about past eras. Influenced by movies,books etc. I developed a unique style characterized by the presence of blood and disaster. Also can be just expression of conditions.

The name "ZOMBIELANDWRLD" reflects my fascination with the zombie genre and its metaphorical potential. Through my paintings, I aim to not just depict zombies, but to use them as a lens through which to examine human nature, societal collapse, and the enduring spirit of survival that is connected to our whimsical world.

I work primarily with oil, acrylic, pencils. to create pieces that are both visually striking and emotionally evocative. Each painting tells a story, inviting the viewer to contemplate the world I've created and to find their own meaning within it.

Thank you for visiting ZOMBIELANDWRLD. I hope you enjoy exploring my artwork.
